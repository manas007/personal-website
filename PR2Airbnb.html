Seattle Airbnb Open Data

A sneak peek into the Airbnb activity in Seattle, WA, US | Jan 2019 - Ongoing

See Data Set

As part of the Airbnb Inside initiative, the dataset describes the listing activity of homestays in Seattle, WA. The 3 datasets are :

  1. Listings, including full descriptions and average review score
  2. Reviews, including unique id for each reviewer and detailed comments
  3. Calendar, including listing id and the price and availability for that day

Project Goal:

  • Pricing guide for hosts depending on popularity during different months/quarters

Statistical Tool: R
