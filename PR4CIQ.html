Client Inquiry Dashboards

Data Science and Process Transformation Team - BNY Mellon | November 2015 - April 2017

Worked with the Digital Pulse, BNY Mellon's platform to capture every event that takes place within the bank irrespective of business, process or client. The platform captured more than 1 billion events per month. My role was to develop analytical reports for Client Inquiry Management System; a particular application within the bank. Key initivaties were:

  1. Understand huge data sets.
  2. Develop queries to filter relevant data for meaningful insights.
  3. Develop Tableau Dashboards and deploy Dashboards to the servers.
  4. Improve performance of legacy dashboards by query tuning and optimization.

Tech Stack

Analytical databases, HP Vertica, Tableau, SQL
